Alex Morgan

Contemporary Oil & Watercolor Artist

Hello! I'm Alex Morgan. My journey with art began as a child, finding solace in the quiet chaos of colors. Today, that childhood curiosity has evolved into a professional pursuit of capturing the fleeting moments of life.

I specialize in oil painting and watercolors, drawing inspiration from nature's textures, human resilience, and the abstract concepts of time and memory. My work is not just about representation, but about evoking a feeling—a memory, a dream, or a moment of stillness.

Each painting creates a dialogue between the viewer and the canvas. I believe art has the power to transform spaces and touch hearts, turning a house into a home.
